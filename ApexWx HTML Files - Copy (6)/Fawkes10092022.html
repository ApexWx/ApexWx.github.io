3-Day Outlook: Tuesday - Thursday

High pressure over the region this morning will drift eastwards into the Atlantic today and tomorrow. A cold front trailing from a strong low in Canada will move through Thursday with showers and thunderstorms for Lancaster and vicinity.  A brief window of opportunity for a few stronger storms to evolve with a couple of strong/marginally severe wind gusts to occur and even a brief tornado, though the probability is low. Showers taper off Thursday night before high pressure builds in Friday.
