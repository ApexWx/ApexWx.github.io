The Week Ahead for Fort Kent & the Saint John Valley by Apex Wx
Updated August 12, 2023 at 9:30 am EDT

Fort Kent, ME, USA — Sun and Moon Times

Moon Phase:🌘 Waning Crescent → 🌑 New Moon at 5:38 am Wed Aug 16

3-Day Outlook: Sat. Aug 12 - Mon. Aug 14

Current NWS Caribou Hazardous Weather Outlook
Click for the latest information

Partly sunny to mostly cloudy skies, but mostly dry conditions, are expected today. Clouds increase tonight as another low approaches from the Great Lakes with an occluded front. Chances for showers increase after midnight. Sunday will be cloudy with rain likely in the morning then showers and thunderstorms in the afternoon, with isolated strong-to-severe storms possible. NWS Caribou states that "gusty winds and small hail will be possible in any storms with freezing levels dropping to near 10K ft."

Showers wind down Sunday evening with some isolated showers possible into the overnight hours with patchy fog across the SJV as the low pressure system moves east into the Maritimes. Partly sunny skies are expected Monday with a slight chance for afternoon showers, and partly cloudy skies Monday night ahead of the next system on Tuesday.

4-7 Day Outlook: Tue. Aug 15 - Fri. Aug 18

Active west-to-east zonal flow will bring systems across the Valley every couple of days during the period. An occluding low pressure system and associated triple point low approaches Tuesday with showers in the morning then rain likely in the afternoon and evening. Rain continues overnight before tapering to showers Wednesday morning.

As the low tracks into the Maritimes, showers linger across the SJV Wednesday with a chance for isolated afternoon thunderstorms. Isolated showers are possible Wednesday night. A weak ridge of high pressure builds in Thursday with partly sunny to partly cloudy skies across the area. Partly cloudy skies Thursday night give way to partly sunny skies Friday as yet another occluding low pressure system approaches from the west bringing a chance for showers, and possibly some thunder, to the Saint John Valley by Friday afternoon. Clouds diminish Friday evening with some lingering scattered showers heading into the overnight hours.

8-14 Day Trends: Sat. Aug 19 -  Fri. Aug 25

Near normal temperatures / Above normal precipitation
