The Week Ahead for Lancaster & Vicinity by Apex Wx
Updated Sunday, January 8 at 10:00 am EST

3-Day Outlook: Sunday - Tuesday

Low pressure will approach from the southwest today and bring increasing clouds this afternoon. Cloudy skies tonight with rain and snow likely after midnight with a slight chance for rain in the morning behind the departing system. Less than 1-inch snow accumulation is anticipated from this system.

Clouds diminish Monday with mostly clear skies developing in the afternoon and continuing into the evening hours with partly cloudy skies after midnight. High pressure builds in Tuesday with partly cloudy skies. Clouds increase Tuesday night as a mostly dry cold front drops down from the north.

4-7 Day Outlook: Wednesday - Saturday

High pressure brings partly cloudy skies Wednesday through Thursday night. Clouds increase Friday as a low pressure system approaches from the west with rain likely Friday afternoon into Friday night. Skies become mostly cloudy Saturday with a chance for rain through the afternoon.
