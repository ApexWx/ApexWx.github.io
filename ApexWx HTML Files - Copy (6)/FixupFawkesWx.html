7-Day Outlook: Mon. June 16 - Sun. June 22
Updated: Monday, June 16, 2025 at 9:16 am EDT

Monday - Wednesday
Low pressure tracks on a stationary boundary over the Mid-Atlantic through this morning. The stationary boundary lifts north as a warm front Tuesday through Tuesday night. Bermuda high pressure sets up offshore Wednesday and Thursday. A pattern change occurs towards the end of the week as a cold front passes through the region Thursday afternoon and night. High pressure returns for Friday and Saturday, possibly followed by a cold front on Sunday.

Today, cloudy skies with scattered showers are expected with less than 1/10-inch rainfall. A high near 70F is expected. Tonight, isolated showers with less than 1/10-inch precipitation expected with a low around 62F.

Mostly cloudy skies are expected Tuesday with a high in the mid-70s. A chance of showers along with isolated afternoon thunderstorms possible. Rainfall amounts  in the 0.01 to 0.09-inch range, unless thunderstorms produce more locally.Tuesday night, cloudy skies with showers likely in the evening along isolated thunder then a chance of showers overnight. A low in the upper 60s with between 1/10 and 1/4-inch rainfall overnight, with higher totals possible in any storms.

Wednesday features partly sunny to partly cloudy skies with a chance of showers and isolated afternoon thunderstorms. Highs climb to the upper 80s with humid conditions producing a heat index into the low 90s. For Wednesday night, mostly clear skies overspread Lancaster with temperatures in the low 70s.

Thursday - Sunday
A cold front approaches and moves through the Lower Susquehanna Valley Thursday into Friday. Mostly sunny skies start the day Thursday with partly cloudy/partly sunny skies in the afternoon with showers likely and scattered thunderstorms. Some storms may be severe. Isolated showers and thunderstorms linger behind the front Thursday night into early Friday with mostly sunny skies overspreading the region as high pressure builds in. Highs Thursday in the upper 80s to near 90F with overnight lows in the mid-60s. Highs Friday reach the mid-80s with overnight lows under partly cloudy skies in the lower 60s.

Saturday and Sunday, high pressure over the southern Mid-Atlantic / Southeast US will dominate the region's weather. Mostly sunny days and partly cloudy nights with highs in the mid-to-upper 80s Saturday and low 90s Sunday. Lows in the upper 60s Saturday night and lower 70s Sunday night. Rain chances are low during the period.
