3-Day Outlook: Wed. Nov. 1 - Fri. Nov. 3

NWS State College Hazardous Weather Conditions

Low pressure and its associated troughs will move away today with high pressure building into the Lower Susquehanna Valley. Mostly clear skies tonight will allow for the first hard freeze of the season with a freeze warning in effect from 11 PM this evening to 10 AM EDT Thursday. Sunny skies continue Thursday with mostly clear skies Thursday night. Mostly sunny skies Friday morning become partly cloudy Friday night as a warm front grazes northern Pennsylvania.
