7-Day Outlook: Fri. Oct. 24 - Thu. Oct. 30
Updated October 24 at 9:35 am EDT

Friday - Sunday
High pressure will build across the Lancaster region today through Sunday. An upper-level trough and weak disturbances could produce increased cloudiness at times. Otherwise, near seasonal conditions are expected this weekend.

Monday - Thursday
Cooler-than-normal weather will continue through next week as a broad upper trough keeps temperatures below average. Early next week looks mostly dry across central Pennsylvania, though a weak storm passing through the Tennessee Valley may bring some clouds and a few light showers near the Maryland border. Breezy conditions will also develop at times. By midweek, another coastal storm could approach the East Coast.
