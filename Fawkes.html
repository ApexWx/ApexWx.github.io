Short-Term Outlook: Thursday - Saturday

Partly sunny skies are expected today as the remnants of Fred lift into New England with a weak trough producing a slight chance for showers and thunderstorms this afternoon. An upper-level trough will still be over the area Friday and through the upcoming weekend, resulting in a daily chance for pop up showers or a few rumbles of thunder. The best chance for precipitation each day will be in the afternoon and early evening.

Medium-Range Outlook: Sunday - Wednesday

The upper trough will remain across the region through the first part of next week before a more zonal flow sets up around midweek. Partly cloudy skies are expected Sunday through Tuesday morning with a daily chance for afternoon showers and thunderstorms. Mostly sunny skies develop Tuesday afternoon and continue through Wednesday with a slight chance for an afternoon shower each day.
