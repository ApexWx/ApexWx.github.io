Current NWS State College, PA Hazardous Weather Outlook

3-Day Outlook: Sat. June 29 - Mon. July 1

Low pressure will track north of Pennsylvania this weekend, with the associated warm front lifting through the state today and the trailing cold front pushing through on Sunday. A large high pressure system will build into the region early in the upcoming week.

For today, mostly cloudy skies with a chance of scattered showers and thunderstorms are expected this afternoon with isolated-to-scattered strong/severe thunderstorms possible. Localized damaging wind gusts are possible this afternoon and evening.

Showers and thunderstorms are likely this evening into the overnight hours. Partly sunny, hot, and quite humid conditions are on tap Sunday with a slight chance of morning showers/thunderstorms then a chance of showers and thunderstorms in the afternoon into the evening. Strong to severe thunderstorms with gusty winds and small hail are possible again on Sunday.

High pressure builds in from the Great Lakes region Monday with mostly sunny skies, cooler, and less humid conditions. Mostly clear skies are expected Monday night into Tuesday.

4-7 Day Outlook: Tue. July 2 - Fri. July 5

Fair and warmer weather is expected Tuesday into at least the first half of Wednesday, as upper level ridging builds over the state and surface high drifts offshore. A weakening disturbance tracks well north of Pennsylvania late Wednesday into Thursday. The dying cold front is likely to sag into the state Thursday and stall out in the vicinity of Pennsylvania through Friday. Falling heights/surging low level moisture ahead of the front could support scattered convection. However, a better chance of showers/thunderstorms area-wide will come with the arrival of the front Thursday into Friday.

For Lancaster and vicinity, Mostly sunny skies are expected Wednesday with a chance of scattered showers and isolated thunderstorms after midnight Wednesday. A chance of shower sand isolated thunderstorms continues into early Thursday. Mostly sunny skies with a chance of afternoon showers and thunderstorms are possible Thursday with scattered showers and thunderstorms overnight into early Friday. Partly sunny skies with a chance of scattered afternoon showers and thunderstorms are possible once again on Friday.
