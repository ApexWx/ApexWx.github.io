3-Day Outlook: Saturday - Monday
High pressure to the north will remain in control of the Valley's weather for the remainder of the weekend with cold, dry air over the region. Today will be sunny with a breezy north wind continuing. Daytime highs only reach the low 20s in Fort Kent with overnight lows dipping back into the single digits above under mostly clear skies.

Mostly sunny skies Sunday morning become partly cloudy in the afternoon as low pressure over Newfoundland moves into eastern New Brunswick. Partly cloudy skies and cold temperatures continue Sunday night.

Partly cloudy to mostly sunny skies Monday will become more cloudy Monday night as a trough circulating around the low to the east carries some moisture over the region.
