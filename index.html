4-7 Day Outlook: Tue. Jan. 9 - Fri. Jan. 12

High pressure will bring partly cloudy to mostly sunny skies Tuesday with clouds increasing Tuesday night as a low pressure system approaches. Snow is expected to overspread the SJV Wednesday morning and continue into the afternoon mixing with and changing to rain Wednesday night before ending Thursday morning. Blustery conditions are expected with this system as well. The development of a secondary triple-point low may affect precipitation type/amounts for northern Maine; however, models are not in agreement on this feature at this time. Mostly cloudy skies are expected Thursday as an associated upper-level trough crosses the region with mostly cloudy skies Thursday night and Friday. Another large storm system may affect the SJV next weekend.
